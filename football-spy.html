FootballSpy

Privacy Policy

Overview

FootballSpy is a social deduction party game where players try to identify the spy while the spy tries to figure out which footballer is being discussed. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

FootballSpy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. The game is played entirely offline on your device. We do not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any third-party data collection tools.

What Is Stored Locally

  • Game preferences and settings
  • Premium purchase status

This data never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

FootballSpy offers optional premium features via in-app purchase. Payment is handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We never have access to your payment information.

Children

FootballSpy is a family-friendly party game. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users.

Data Deletion

Since no personal data is collected or stored on external servers, there is no data to delete. You can remove all local data by deleting the app from your device.

Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected here with an updated date.
