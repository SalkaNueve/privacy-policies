Privacy Policy

Effective March 1, 2026
In short

OnePercent stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information. Your habits, progress, and profile never leave your phone.

About OnePercent

OnePercent is a habit-tracking app built around the philosophy of 1% daily improvement. The app helps you log daily habits, track your compound progress over time, and celebrate milestones along the way.

OnePercent is developed and published by SalkaNueve. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal data. All information you enter into OnePercent — including your name, habits, daily logs, and preferences — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics infrastructure. Your data exists only on your iPhone.

Information stored on your device

The following information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere:

Profile information: Your first name, age range, and gender (all optional except first name). This is used only to personalize greetings within the app.

Habit data: Your habit titles, daily log entries, progress calculations, streak counts, and milestone achievements.

Preferences: Your selected theme, reminder times, and notification settings.

Widget data: A small amount of data is shared between the app and the home screen widget via Apple's App Groups. This data remains on your device.

Subscriptions and payments

OnePercent offers an optional premium subscription processed entirely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit framework. We do not process, store, or have access to any payment information, credit card numbers, or Apple ID details.

All subscription management, billing, and payment processing is handled by Apple. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information about how Apple handles your purchase data.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, OnePercent schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are processed entirely by iOS and are not routed through any external server. We do not receive notification tokens or push notification data.

Third-party services

OnePercent does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services. We do not use Firebase, Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar tools.

The only third-party interaction is with Apple's StoreKit for subscription purchases, and Apple's WidgetKit for home screen widgets — both are native iOS frameworks.

Data sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with anyone. Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no ability to access or share it even if we wanted to.

Data deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Reset all data" option in the app's Settings. This permanently removes all habits, progress, and profile information from your device.

Uninstalling the app will also remove all associated data from your device.

Children's privacy

OnePercent does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The app does not require or encourage the input of personal information beyond a first name for greeting personalization.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
